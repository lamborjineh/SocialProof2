🗺️ My Map — How it works

Build your own knowledge map about media literacy topics.

  • ➕Add a parent node — Use the Add Node panel on the right, or click + Add parent node inside the panel to create a top-level topic.
  • 🔗Add sub-nodes — Click any node on the canvas to open its detail panel, then click + Add sub-node here to branch out from it.
  • ✋Drag nodes — Click and drag any node to reposition it. Positions are saved automatically and persist after refresh.
  • 🔍Pan & zoom — Click and drag the background to pan the canvas. Scroll (or pinch) to zoom in and out.
  • ✏️Edit or delete — Hover over any node to see the delete button, or open the node and click Edit this node to change its label, shape, icon, or colors.
  • 🎨Box & text color — When editing a node, Box Color controls the border/glow accent and Text Color controls the label text — they are fully independent.
  • 📄Article / Notes — Use the Article / Notes field to attach long-form text, pasted articles, or detailed notes to any node. Tap the header in the panel to expand or collapse it.
  • ✦AI suggestions — Open any node and click ✦ AI suggest a node to get AI-powered ideas for what to explore next.
